Ever thought about converting any typed piece of text into your handwriting? Well, with the help of hanwriter.in it is possible! Once you are on the website, follow along to see how you can convert any text into your handwriting in just a click! 


	

Step 1: Type or paste any text into the text box and click next.

Step 2: Selecting a handwriting. Selecting a handwriting can be done in 2 ways. You could either chose a default handwriting or add your own handwriting. If you want to use a default handwriting, click on one of the templates shown and viola the website will generate the handwriting for your typed text! You can also download this image for your own projects.

Step 3: And here you go. You have the the text in your own handwriting viola handwriter.in.

 • Another way to generate handwritten text is by using your own handwriting. To do this let’s go back to the home page and download the template. 
You will notice that it has empty grid boxes for you to fill in with your own handwriting.
Make sure that you fill in the template in the same order as shown in this image.
You will notice that the template has numbers, lowercase alphabets, upper case alphabets, punctuation and some other special characters. 
For best results we suggest entering 2 more sets of the same characters into the template.

• Now you’re all set to generate your own handwriting!! Head back to the website and upload the template you just created.
